# Code of Conduct

ASTER is intended to be a professional, practical OSS project. Contributors are expected to keep discussion technical, respectful, and focused on improving the software.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, threats, discriminatory remarks, deliberate disruption, publishing private information, and posting secrets or production user data.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and contributions that violate this standard, and may restrict participation when needed to protect the project and its users.
